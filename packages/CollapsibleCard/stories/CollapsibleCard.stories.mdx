## Usage

### Basic

Below is an example of a basic collapsible card:

```jsx
<CollapsibleCard>
  <CollapsibleCard.Header>
    <CollapsibleCard.Segment>This is the header. Click to reveal body.</CollapsibleCard.Segment>
  </CollapsibleCard.Header>
  <CollapsibleCard.Body>This is the body.</CollapsibleCard.Body>
</CollapsibleCard>
```
