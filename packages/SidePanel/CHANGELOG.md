## [0.3.16] - 2020-04-16

## [0.3.24] - 2020-06-02

### Changed

- <SidePanel> no longer has default padding or margin. Instead make use of the <SidePanel.Content> sub component to apply the padding to a section of the SidePanel. (https://github.com/KaanDarcey).
